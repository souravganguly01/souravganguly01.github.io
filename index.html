About

I am a Postdoctoral Research Fellow in the Department of Earth and Planetary Sciences at Weizmann Institute of Science, Israel. My research is broadly focused on low temperature geochemical processes that shapes the Earth surface, its hydrosphere and near-subsurface.

My work blends real-world observations, geochemical and isotopic analyses of water, sediment and rock samples,, and geochemial modeling
